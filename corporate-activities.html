Corporate Baking

"Never, ever underestimate the importance of having fun."

- Randy Pausch

- MASTER CHEF -

Can you handle the heat?

Assume the role of the apprentice during the first half of the lesson. Learn from the Chef as he conducts a demonstration on 2 desserts. It would be difficult for any one member to memorize the entire process and ingredients required, so some planning and delegation is required.

Teams are encouraged to show off their creativity by adding personal touches to the dish and it will take everyone’s combined effort to create delicious. Apart from the chef, team members will be taste-testing other group’s creations and the team with the most “likes” will be declared the winner

- SCAVENGER HUNT -

Only the fastest will survive!

Unlike other challenges, teams start this one off at a “shopping mall” and a set budget. They will be given a unique recipe and limited time to gather all missing ingredients.

Each member will also be given a secret challenge to complete while collecting these ingredients. Performance of this task is not easy and teams are advised to spend some time strategizing. After the time is up, teams will gather back at their workstations and the baking will commence.

Each team must complete their dish within the given time and at the end of the session, our chef will hold a dessert-judging session and the best-rated team gets to win an exclusive hamper (and eternal bragging rights).

- SWEET WHISPERS -

Shushhhhhhh...

Teams of 4, consisting of 2 Listeners and 2 Whisperers will compete to become the Royal Chef, with only the cream of the crop making it. Listeners will face with each other in challenges while the Whisperers aim to steal the recipes from the King, played by one of our in-house chefs!

Once that is done, the 4 of you will rendezvous and recreate the King’s favourite dishes. When all that is done, teams will present their work to the King for judging. Only one team can emerge victorious and this is a survival of the fittest in its rawest form!
