WHY Lorong Bakes

Lorong means “alley” or “lane” in Malay and reflects our local roots while Bakes conveys what we do as simply as possible.
It is also reflective of our first ever office/studio which we opened in 2009 in Lorong Chuan.
Even after moving to our new headquarters in Orchard Central, we’ve never forgotten our humble beginnings hence the name.

Mission

Lorong Bakes was created with the intention to bring people closer together through the art of baking and making desserts. We offer hands-on baking classes for everyone and our mission is to make baking fun and simple. We believe that age is just a number and experience can be gained through learning. As long as you have the interest and a positive learning attitude, we are more than happy to welcome you to join our community!

Team

Our impressive 2000 square feet, fully air-conditioned, open concept studio is staffed by a team of highly skilled and experienced chefs. The studio is also equipped with the latest commercial and domestic appliance for creating high quality desserts. We can accommodate up to 60 participants and there’s even an outdoor area for our customers to enjoy the fruits of their labour after the classes. Our impressive studio facilities have welcomed both international and local celebrities such as Anthony Bourdain, James Martin and Michelle Chong.

Passion

Our passion for baking, artful desserts and dedication to quality and innovation remains the motivation behind every classes and treats we bake. We take pride in seeing how versatile and creative our participants have become after attending our baking classes.
