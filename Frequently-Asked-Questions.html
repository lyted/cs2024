Frequently Asked Questions

For clearing any doubts you may have.

1) Do you have vegetarian/vegan friendly food?

Our menu is not fixed so we cannot guarantee that we will be baking vegetarian food.

However, we will state if the food is vegetarian or vegan.

2) Do you have classes for halal food?

We maintain a Halal certified studio and the ingredients we use in class are Halal as well.

3) Can I bring home what I bake?

Yes, you are free to do so as it is the work of your hard labour.

4) Can I use Skillsfuture Credit to pay for my classes?

Not at the moment as we are not Skillsfuture accredited. However we are working towards that.

5) Can I go for more than 1 trial lesson?

We have to be fair to everyone, thus you can only attend 1 free trial lesson.

6) Who will be our chef instructors?

Different chefs will be assigned to teach different baking classes.

However, rest assured that all chefs are qualified to cook and teach.

7) Do I need to bring anything to class?

Just yourself, a willingness to learn and a positive attitude.

We provide all the equipment you need for all classes.

8) What should I wear for the classes?

Please wear covered shoes for your own safety.

The dress code is casual and comfortable unless otherwise advised.

9) I am unable to make it for a class, what should I do?

You can either cancel or change the date of your class.

However, please note that we will only allow you to attend another class if there is a slot open. If not, you will need to cancel it.

For cancellations, you will be refunded the fee provided it's carried at least a week before the event. If not, you will only be refunded 50%.

10) What is the skill level of the class?

The skill level of the class will be stated in it's description so customers will know what they are signing up for.
